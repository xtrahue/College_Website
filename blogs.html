IEM UEM MARATHON

IEM-UEM Kolkata Marathon 2022, a unique marathon organized by college students of Institute of Engineering & Management, Kolkata, successfully organized their fifth instalment on Sunday, 27th March. The Covid Warriors, as they call themselves, succeeded in their mission of spreading awareness about the new normal and helping people adapt to it, with over a 1000 participation slots being sold out a week in advance.The highlight of the event being participation of people like, a person running with prosthetic leg, a lady completing 10K with her baby in a stroller, a 3 year kid completing 5KM and a blind guy finishing his run. The event consisted of three runs, a half marathon, a 10K run and a 5K run.

Rishikesh Chakraborty(men category) with a timing of 1 for and 14 minutes and Mukti Bayen(women category) with a timing of 1 hour 32 minutes won the half marathon. Soumen Ghosh(mens, 32 minutes) and Sanchita Mondel(womens, 45 minutes) won the 10 K run and Rohit Prasad(menâ€™s, 17 minutes) and Priti Prahapati(womens, 22 minutes) won in 5K run. Cash prizes for winners and medals were there for all finishers. The best part of the event as per the runners feedback were the ample cheering from the college volunteers and more then enough hydration with a water booth at every 500 metres, making sure that the runner is fully motivated through the run, and the same happened till the last person was on the track.
